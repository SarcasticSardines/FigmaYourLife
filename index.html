About Me!


Hi, my name is Cinnamoroll! I was born way above the clouds in the sky, where I lived with Mama Sky and Papa Sun, and some of my cloud siblings too. One day I accidentally fell down, and I fell all the way down to a place called Shukuru Town. I was lost and all alone, but then I found Cafe Cinnamon and met Ana! Anna owns the cafe, and she named me "Cinnamon" because my tail looks like a cinnamon bun. She's so nice, she even made me the Cafe's mascot!

I met so many new people because of Ana, so now I have lots of new friends to help out! Sometimes I get shy, but being around my friends helps me feel better. I love hanging out with my friends and flying around town with my big ears to look for adventures and fun! Don't be fooled by my big ears though, I'm a flying dog, not a bunny! My favorite thing to eat are the fresh warm cinnamon buns Anna makes! I also love to help people out, but sometimes I work too hard and fall asleep on customer's laps...
